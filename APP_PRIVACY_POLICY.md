# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 1/2/2026**

## Overview

This app is designed to allow users to control their PC via Bluetooth. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains how your data is handled.


## Information Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.
- No account is required
- No analytics are used
- No data is transmitted to any server
- No information is stored on your device beyond what Android itself requires for normal operation


## Bluetooth Usage

The app uses Bluetooth solely to communicate with your PC.

Bluetooth communication happens locally between your Android device and your computer.

No Bluetooth data is collected, logged, or transmitted elsewhere.


## Third‑Party Services

This app does not use any third‑party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.


## Children’s Privacy

Since the app collects no data, it is safe for users of all ages.


## Changes to This Policy

If the privacy policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here or within the app.


## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer at https://github.com/patrikflorek/Kurza/issues
